Welcome to NailCheck!

At NailCheck, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the detection and diagnosis of nail diseases using the power of machine learning (ML) technology. Our web application provides a user-friendly and efficient way to identify various nail conditions, empowering individuals to take control of their nail health.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the early detection and prevention of nail diseases by leveraging the capabilities of ML algorithms. We strive to make nail health assessment accessible, accurate, and convenient for everyone. By providing reliable and timely information, we aim to improve overall well-being and promote proactive nail care.

  • Our ML models are designed to deliver accurate and reliable results, providing insights into potential nail diseases with high precision.
  • User-Friendly Interface: We prioritize usability, ensuring that our web application is intuitive and easy to navigate. Simply follow the instructions to capture and upload images of your nails, and our system will handle the rest.
